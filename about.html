Clothing, Inc.

South African company headquartered in Pretoria, CBD. It was founded in 2021 by Vinolia Masango,
a sneaker fanitic, and her current friend Neo Ngobeni. They opened their first retail outlet in
2022 and launched different brands of sneakers in 2022. The company was renamed Sneakers, Inc.,
in 2021 and went public a year later.
By start of 2022, Sneakers, Inc. had retail outlets and distributors in more than 5 cities in South Africa,
and its new fresh brands will recognized throughout the world.


Part of Sneakers, Inc.’s success is owed to endorsements by such Brand Ambassadors and Actors as
Thabiso Baholo, Thato Mashinini, Naledi Mogadime, and Nefisa Mkhabela.
The SneakerTown chain stores, the first of which opened at the beginning of 2022, pay tribute to these
and other company spokespersons while offering consumers a full range of Sneaker, Inc. products.
